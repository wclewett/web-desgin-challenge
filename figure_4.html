Latitude vs. Wind Speed (mph)


Scatterplot of Latitude vs. Wind Speed, n = 550.

Wind Speed and Latitude do not seem to share any linear relationship. To keep congruency in our study, we again split up the data into a Northern and Southern Hemisphere at the Equator.


Northern and Southern Hemisphere, Linear Regressions

Splitting the dataset into two hemispheres did not merit any plausable correlations between Wind Speed and Latitude. It would be best to find a new metric if we wanted to further study how geolocation impacts Wind Speed.
